\section*{Key points}

\begin{enumerate}
  \item Equivalent sources methods in Cartesian coordinates using map projections
    introduce distortions at continental or global scales.
  \item Our spherical dual-layer gradient-boosted equivalent sources method is
    able to process over 1.5 million data points on modest hardware.
  \item The three components of the anomalous magnetic field can be
    independently and accurately recovered from total-field anomaly data.
\end{enumerate}
